Dragon Fruit

Dragon fruit, with its vibrant pink skin and unique, mildly sweet flavor, is a nutrient-rich tropical fruit known for its striking appearance and health benefits.

Banana

A banana is a long, curved fruit with a soft, creamy flesh encased in a yellow, sometimes speckled peel. It has a sweet flavor and is rich in potassium and fiber. Bananas are commonly enjoyed fresh, added to smoothies, or used in baking.

Pear

A pear is a sweet, juicy fruit with a rounded base that narrows towards the top. It has smooth skin that can be green, yellow, red, or brown, and tender, flavorful flesh inside. Pears are enjoyed fresh, cooked, or in various dishes and desserts.

Mango

A mango is a tropical fruit with sweet, juicy orange flesh and a tough skin that ripens from green to red or yellow. It has a large pit in the center and a flavor that combines citrus and peach notes.

Pineapple

A pineapple is a tropical fruit with a spiky, tough outer skin and sweet, juicy flesh inside. It has a distinctive crown of spiky green leaves and a vibrant yellow interior, making it both visually appealing and deliciously tangy.
